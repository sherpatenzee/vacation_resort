The Resort

Overlooking Lake Travis, Sherpa Resort and Spa features beautiful accommodations amidst the natural landscape of Texas Hill Country's pristine setting. Boasting a boutique spa with significant lake views, a pool playground and extensive meeting and event space with impressive lake vistas throughout, the resort offers an abundance to discover, with close proximity to championship golf courses, premier wine tours, outdoor recreation and water sports.
